Some symptoms that are most symnonomus with a common cold to which you may be experiencing are a runny nose, a stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat,fever, and or coughing.

Solutions

Some solutions to which could help relieve this problem, as there is no direct medicine to out right cure a common cold unfortunatley, are as follows:

  • Tylenol: Tylenol is medicine that comes in pill and or liquid form that will reduce fevers
  • Advil: Advil are also medicine pills will reduce inflammation,fever, and pain.
  • Rest: As mentioned above due to the nature of the common cold there's not any direct medicine to which will make it cease so if you're symptoms don't meet any of the previous solutions it's best to simply get some rest and let your immune system deal with the cold.
