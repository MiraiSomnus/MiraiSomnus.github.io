Pollen Allergy

Symptoms

The symptoms you will experience if you have a pollen allergy are a sneezing, runny and or stuffy nose, itchy eyes, and a itchy throat

Solutions

Solutions that will help you deal with the effects of an pollen allergy are:

  • Zyrtec: Zyrtec are medicine pills is an antihestamine that covers and treats all of symptoms listed above.
  • Flonase: Flonase is a nasal spray that relieves congestion in the nose whilst also putting an ened to sneezing, runny noses, and itchy eyes and throat.
